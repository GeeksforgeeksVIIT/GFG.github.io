Digital Elevate-Unlocking the secrets of LinkedIn’s Growth

GFG-VIIT hosted a compelling session titled "Digital Elevate - Unlocking the Secrets of LinkedIn’s Growth" on December 9, 2023. Led by Mr. Yash Awidra, Sr. Consultant at Mercedes Benz with a remarkable LinkedIn following of 155k, the session attracted 510 enthusiastic attendees from both institutions. Mr. Awidra delivered actionable insights, strategies, and tools to optimize LinkedIn profiles and expand professional networks effectively. Participants gained invaluable knowledge on leveraging LinkedIn's features for personal branding and networking, fostering engaging discussions and receiving practical tips for enhancing online visibility.This collaborative event exemplified a commitment to continuous learning and personal growth within the GFG-VIIT and LIET College communities.

Three90 Challenge

The GFG-VIIT's awareness program for the GFG Three90 Challenge, led by core members of GFG SC - VIIT 2023 on January 29, 2024, saw an impressive turnout of 480 participants. This event significantly heightened awareness and motivation among learners, leading to increased enrollment and completion rates of courses on the GFG website, fostering a culture of goal-oriented learning within the community. As an added incentive, participants who completed the challenge were rewarded with exciting GFG swags such as stickers, pens, and keychains, further motivating them to engage with the platform and continue their learning journey. This initiative not only promoted skill development but also encouraged active participation and engagement within the GFG-VIIT community, reinforcing the importance of continuous learning and self-improvement.
